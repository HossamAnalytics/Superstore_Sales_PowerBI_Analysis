## üìÇ Dashboard Structure (4 Pages)

### 1. Summary Analysis
Provides a high-level overview of business health.
* **Yearly Sales Growth:** Tracks running totals and sales trends from 2022 to 2025.
* **Geographical Insights:** Performance breakdown across top countries (France, Germany) and cities.

### 2. Product Root Cause Analysis
Focuses on diagnostic analytics to identify performance gaps.
* **Root Cause Discovery:** Detailed analysis of low-performing regions like Denmark (Copenhagen & Odense).
* **Product Performance:** Highlights top-selling items such as Apple and Nokia Smart Phones.

### 3. Customer Segmentation (RFM Analysis)
An advanced analytical study categorizing customers into 11 segments based on their behavior.
* **Segments Identified:** Includes Champions, Loyal Customers, At Risk, and Hibernating segments.
* **Retention Trends:** Tracks new vs. repetitive customer behavior over a 4-year period.

### 4. Forecasting Analysis
Leverages predictive analytics for future business planning.
* **2026 Projections:** Forecasts Total Sales and Net Profit for the next year based on historical trends.
* **What-If Scenario:** Features a growth rate toggle for dynamic simulation.
